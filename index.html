Interesting facts about camel

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat

Interesting facts about pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

Interesting facts about teddy

  • The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846
  • The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906

Interesting facts about cicada

  • There are around 3,000 cicada species
  • they vary in size from 0.75 to 2.25 in (2.2 to 5.5 cm) long
  • Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.

Interesting facts about panda

  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears
  • Cubs are well protected in their first month

Interesting facts about chameleon

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues
